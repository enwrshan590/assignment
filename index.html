About Us

Our mission is to change the food system that affects all of us. Food is political, the choices we make about food resonate through our community, economy, and planet. In 1994, Organica founded it's restaurant on this ideology. We hope to provide our customers with healthy, organic and tasty food.

Our Story

We are pround to be partnered up with some of London's local farmers. We source organic food first and go local whenever possible. Non of our food is genetically modified and is garunteed fresh and tastey. Organic farming practices are designed to encourage soil and water conservation and reduce pollution.

Menu

Organica offers gourmet, gluten & sugar free, vegan dishes to invigorate & cleanse your body, mind & soul! Our top chefs have prepared beautiful and complex dishes to meet anyones palate. Our food is created for people who care about what they eat. It is clean and satisfying. For us, healthy means simple, fresh ingredients that are high in nutritional value. Our menu supports a balanced diet with lots of veggies, lean meats and fish, whole intact grains and heart-healthy nuts and fats. Click through our tabs below to see some of our menu options.

Our Chefs

Chef Michael Allemeier

For more than 20 years, Chef Michael's enthusiasm, inspired creativity and commitment to healthy eating has provided a winning recipe for Organica. He has cooked in South Africa, Italy, United States and now in Canada. He is an in-demand instructor that will be also participating in our cooking classes. He hopes to bring you the most beautiful food you have ever seen.

Chef Maria Marte

Classically trained in French cuisine at Le Cordon Bleu, Ottawa, Maria holds Le Grand Diplôme, a blue-ribboned’ for diplomas in pastry and cuisine. She is a Quebec native and believes food tastes best made from scratch, using local and seasonal ingredients. Her classes emphasize fundamental cooking techniques that can be applied to any recipe.

Chef Michael Mina

Passionate, driven and undeniably talented, Chef Micheal Mina started his culinary career when he was only 16 when he working as a dishwasher at a local restaurant in Hamilton. Micheal decided to turn his dream into reality and accomplished this when he decided to enter the culinary world by completing the culinary Program at Fanshawe here in London
